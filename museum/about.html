關於 StarRiver MuseumAbout StarRiver Museum

一項以 VR、網頁與空間敘事為媒介的數位策展計畫。

A digital curation project working across VR, the web, and spatial narrative.

Museum 預計在明年展開更完整的計畫。現階段以季前展公開兩件網頁展覽，先建立觀看、研究與策展的基礎。

A fuller museum program is planned for next year. The current preseason presents two web exhibitions that establish an early foundation for viewing, research, and curation.

季前展與策展研究Preseason exhibitions and curatorial research

現有展品是未來常設展的先行版本，不代表完整美術館已正式開館。

The current works are precursors to a future permanent program, not a claim that the full museum has opened.

關注方向Current Focus

空間、地景與觀看Space, Landscape, and Viewing

現階段關注虛擬空間的尺度與光線、地景如何被轉譯，以及觀眾如何在數位展覽中閱讀與移動。

The current focus is on scale and light in virtual space, the translation of landscape, and how visitors read and move through digital exhibitions.
