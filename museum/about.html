關於美術館About the Museum

StarRiver Museum 將 VR、網頁與空間敘事視為展覽媒介，而不是單純的展示平台。

StarRiver Museum treats VR, the web, and spatial narrative as exhibition media rather than neutral display platforms.

美術館關注台灣地景、移動經驗、尺度感、科學概念與虛擬空間如何交會，並把這些內容轉譯成網頁展覽、數位展品、策展文章與未來虛擬展場入口。

The museum focuses on where Taiwanese landscapes, movement, scale, scientific concepts, and virtual space intersect, translating them into web exhibitions, digital exhibits, curatorial writing, and future virtual gallery entry points.
