StarRiver Museum

StarRiver Museum 是一座以 VR 空間、台灣地景、科學概念與互動展覽為核心的線上美術館。

StarRiver Museum is an online museum centered on VR space, Taiwanese landscapes, scientific concepts, and interactive exhibitions.

這裡整理展覽資訊、網頁展覽、策展方向與未來虛擬展場入口，讓觀眾先理解這座美術館關注什麼，再進入各個展覽與研究模組。

This entry gathers exhibition information, web exhibitions, curatorial direction, and future virtual gallery access, so visitors can first understand what this museum presents before entering specific exhibitions and study modules.

About The MuseumAbout The Museum

美術館關注的方向What the museum is concerned with

StarRiver Museum 以虛擬策展為核心，將地景、科學概念與空間敘事轉譯為可步行、可觀看、可互動的展覽形式。

StarRiver Museum centers virtual curation, translating landscapes, scientific concepts, and spatial narratives into exhibitions that can be walked through, viewed, and interacted with.

這座美術館關注 VR / Web 作為展覽媒介，也關注台灣地景如何被重新組織成虛擬空間、展場結構與觀看方法。

The museum treats VR and the web as exhibition media, while also studying how Taiwanese landscapes can be reorganized into virtual spaces, exhibition structures, and ways of looking.
