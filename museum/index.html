VR Gallery Spatial System

VR 美術館的完整設計系統。空間層涵蓋材質、光源人因、留白、展品預留量體與曲線幾何；標識層定義字級、安裝高度與尋路色碼。以 130 cm avatar 為基準。

A complete design system for VR gallery environments. The spatial layer covers materials, light, ergonomics, whitespace, reserve volumes, and curve geometry; the signage layer defines typographic scale, mounting heights, and wayfinding colour codes. Calibrated to a 130 cm avatar.
