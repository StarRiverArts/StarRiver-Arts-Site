季前展Preseason

StarRiver Museum 正式開館前的網頁展覽季。

A season of web exhibitions before the full opening of StarRiver Museum.

現階段以兩件數位作品測試虛擬展場的尺度、光線、標識與觀看方式，作為未來常設展及數位策展方向的先行版本。

Two digital works currently examine scale, light, signage, and modes of viewing in virtual galleries, serving as early forms of the museum's future permanent and digitally curated program.
