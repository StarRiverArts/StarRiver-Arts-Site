支持美術館Support the Museum

目前支持方式以合作、提案、贊助洽談與技術協作為主，先不把 Museum 對外寫成金流頁。

For now, support begins through collaboration, proposals, sponsorship discussions, and technical cooperation, rather than presenting the public museum site as a payment page.

如果你想參與 StarRiver Museum 的展覽製作、研究整理或跨域合作，這一頁先提供清楚的對話方向，再由聯絡頁承接具體溝通。

If you want to take part in exhibition production, research organization, or cross-disciplinary collaboration at StarRiver Museum, this page first outlines the paths for conversation before the contact page carries the concrete exchange.

There is no online donation or automated patronage flow yet. All support begins through email or phone contact.

建議來信內容What to include in a message

為了讓洽談更有效率，來信時可以先說明合作類型、時間點、資源條件與期望回應方式。

To make conversations more efficient, it helps to state the collaboration type, timeline, available resources, and preferred response mode in your message.

目前這一頁先作為「支持美術館」的正式殼體，避免把支持等同於單一捐款按鈕，也保留後續擴充金流或會員方案的空間。

For now this page acts as the formal shell for supporting the museum, avoiding the idea that support equals a single donation button while leaving room for future patronage or membership models.
