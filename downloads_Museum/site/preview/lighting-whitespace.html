Light Whitespace · 藝術品周圍光線

when to dim · when to wash

A · Pitch-black surround

全黑包圍・劇場聚光
Surround drops to ≤ 0.5 lux. Subject is the only light source visually. Avatar's pupils dilate; transitions to next room MUST include an adaptation buffer.
use
1 hero piece per gallery · video / lightbox / cubicle works
cap
≤ 1 room per visit
buffer
2 m dim antechamber before & after

B · Dim silhouette

微亮輪廓・神祕氣氛
Surround at 2–8 lux. Other works read as silhouettes — present but unfocused. The current work is unambiguous; the others lie in wait.
use
narrative galleries · sequential reveal · contemplative
cap
≤ 3 rooms per visit
contrast
subject 25–50× surround

C · Even wash

均勻照明・全民共見
Surround at 120–200 lux. All works compete equally. Use when the COLLECTION is the point, not any single piece.
use
didactic rooms · group shows · study spaces
contrast
subject only 2–3× surround
never
after a pitch-black room — break the rhythm

D · Gradient transition

漸層過渡・節奏轉換
Long room with surround sliding from pitch-black to wash. Subjects sit at every brightness step — they share the room but live in different lighting climates.
use
transition halls · "from dusk to noon" curatorial themes
length
≥ 8 m to read as gradient, not as patchy lighting
steps
4 brightness zones max
strategy
prev: pitch-black
prev: silhouette
prev: wash
prev: gradient
