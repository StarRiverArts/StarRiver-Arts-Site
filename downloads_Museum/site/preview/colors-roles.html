Role · 語義對應

when to use

env · 周邊

Visible nature outside the gallery shell — foliage seen through openings, terrain, sky bleed. Never an interior surface.

body · 本體

The gallery itself — primary walls, floors, ceilings, plinths. 60–80% of any view should fall on body.

cool · 冷調

Contemplative rooms, north-facing daylight, water features, monochrome works. Used to slow the visitor.

warm · 暖調

Intimate rooms, south-facing rooms, wood floors, candle-light moments. Used to invite proximity.
