Grouping · 群組與呼吸

2-3-2 rhythm

Within a group

40 cm pair-spacing maximum. Works read as one composition. Three works max per group.

Between groups

≥ 180 cm breath. Beat changes — a different idea, period, or material begins.

Rhythm

Aim for varied group sizes — 2-3-2 reads more musical than 2-2-2-2. Avoid all-pairs.

The 留白 rule

If a room has ≥ 12 works on one wall, you have skipped the breath rule. Cut works or split the wall with a partition.
