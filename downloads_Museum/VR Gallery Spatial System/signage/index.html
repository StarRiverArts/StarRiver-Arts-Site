Typographic & Signage System

虛擬實境美術館・字體與標識設計準則 · 14 cards · 4 tiers
Overview · 總覽
a layer on the Spatial System

This system governs how words appear in the VR gallery — wayfinding, signage, and lettering that climbs from flat typesetting all the way up to spatial installation. It is a visual layer that sits on top of the VR Gallery Spatial System, inheriting its four role colours, its 130 cm avatar eye-line, its 10 cm grid, and its bilingual, museum-quiet voice.

本系統規範文字在 VR 美術館中的呈現——導覽、招牌、字體，從平面排版一路升級為空間裝置。它是站在「空間設計準則」之上的視覺層，繼承四色角色盤、130 公分 avatar 視線、10 公分網格，以及雙語、museum-quiet 的語氣。

The spectrum · 升級階梯
1 card · the spine

The organising idea: a single word climbs four states, leaving the page a little more at each rung and inheriting one rule from the spatial system. Everything else in this system is an application of one rung.

Tier 0 · Type scale · 字級
1 card

Signage type is sized by cap-height in cm and verified against viewing distance (cap ≈ distance ⁄ 200). Six steps from the 60 cm building sign to the 1.5 cm caption.

Tier 0 · Bilingual & numerals · 雙語・數字
1 card

Which language leads (culturally-specific → 中文 first; technical-international → English first), and the numeral-led directory built on tabular numerals — the backbone of every floor and zone sign.

Tier 1 · Main lockup · 主招牌
1 card

The building identity: one volumetric lockup in extruded white letters on the limestone body wall — plus three configuration variants (stacked / single-line / monogram).

Tier 1 · Relief & mount · 浮凸・安裝
1 card

The five relief depths (flush 0.3 → sculpt 60 cm) that the spectrum climbs, and the mount-height elevation — every sign type's centroid plotted against the 130 cm avatar eye-line.

Tier 2 · Directional · 方向指引
1 card

Decision-point signs: one overhead bar (max three destinations + one arrow), with chevron, floor-painted, and freestanding-blade variants.

Tier 2 · Directory · 樓層總覽
1 card

The numeral-led floor directory: a single dark board, one row inverted for the current floor, amenities as line pictograms — never emoji.

Tier 2 · Map · 地圖總覽
1 card

The total-overview map redraws the spatial system's circular building as a colour-coded plan — fan wings keyed to zone colour, atrium at centre, north up, you-are-here in red.

Tier 2 · Labels · 展品標籤
1 card

Reading text mapped onto the spatial system's three information ranges — near label (80) / mid context (180) / far ambient sky-text (400 cm) — each with its own weight, contrast, and persistence.

Tier 3 · Emissive · 發光體
1 card

The letter as a light source, bound by the spatial system's emission budget (spill ≤ 20% of the wall). Glow tints + intensity bands; one loud letter per room.

Tier 3 · Volumetric · 量體雕塑
1 card

At sculptural relief the letter becomes an exhibit — it occupies floor volume and is treated as a reserve (reserve L), with circulation clearance on every side.

Tier 3 · Projection · 投影／百葉
1 card

Two ways the word dematerialises: suspended slat letters (solid head-on, dissolving off-axis) and projected text that re-aims toward the nearest viewer.

Tier 3 · Negative form · 留白負形
1 card

The letter is removed from the wall, not added to it — the void carries the meaning and frames the garden or recess behind. 留白 taken literally.

Application · 實景套用
1 card

Drop a screenshot of your own VR world into each frame and place the system on top — one sign per tier, nothing more. 把你自己的 VR 世界截圖拖入，再把系統疊上去，每階各一件、絕不多。

About · 關於
relationship to VGSS
Two systems, one gallery. The VR Gallery Spatial System (VGSS) governs the space itself — materials, light, scale, whitespace. VGTS governs the words inside it. They share tokens: this system's signage_tokens.css @imports VGSS's colors_and_type.css, so the four role colours, the 130 cm avatar, the type families and the 10 cm grid stay identical across both.

兩套系統、同一座美術館：空間準則管「空間本體」，本準則管「空間裡的文字」。兩者共用 token——本系統的 signage_tokens.css 直接 @import 空間系統的 colors_and_type.css。
