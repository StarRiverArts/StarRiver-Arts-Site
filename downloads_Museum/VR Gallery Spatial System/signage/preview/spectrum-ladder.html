Spectrum · 升級階梯

one word · four states

The same word climbs four states. Each rung leaves the page a little more and inherits one rule from the spatial system. 同一個字爬升四階，每一階離開平面更多一點，並繼承空間系統的一條規則。

光
0Typeset排版
Flat on a surface, relief ≈ 0. The word does one job: be read. 貼在表面、近乎零浮凸。字只做一件事——被閱讀。
relief 0.3 cm inherits 留白・字級
光
1Relief立體招牌
Extruded off the wall. It casts a shadow, takes a material, becomes an object. 擠出牆面、投下陰影、擁有材質——成為物件。
relief 2–20 cm inherits 材質×光・曲線
光
2Environmental環境標識
Leaves the wall to occupy the room — overhead, on the floor, freestanding. It orients the body. 離開牆面進入空間——懸於頭頂、落於地面、獨立站立，用來定位身體。
mount 0 / 130 / 215 cm inherits 人因・視錐・浮高
光
3Installation裝置藝術
The word becomes the artwork — it emits light, hangs, projects, or is carved as void. It no longer labels the space; it is an exhibit. 字成為作品本身——發光、懸吊、投影或挖空。它不再標示空間，而是一件展品。
emission ≤ 20% wall inherits 發光預算・粒子
