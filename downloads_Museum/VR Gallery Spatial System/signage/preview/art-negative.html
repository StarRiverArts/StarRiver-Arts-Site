Tier 3 · the void is the letter

The letter is not added to the wall — it is removed from it. The void carries the meaning, framing the garden or the shadowed recess behind. This is 留白 taken literally: the empty shape is the message. 字不是加在牆上，而是從牆上「挖去」。負形承載意義，框住身後的庭園或陰影。
