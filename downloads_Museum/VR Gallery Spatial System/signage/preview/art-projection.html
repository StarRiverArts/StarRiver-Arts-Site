Projection · 投影／百葉

Tier 3 · read-angle dependent

Two ways the word dematerialises. Slat letters are built from suspended horizontal bars — solid head-on, dissolving as you pass. Projected text is cast onto floor or wall and re-aims toward the nearest viewer. 兩種「去物質化」：百葉字由懸吊橫桿組成，正面實、側面散；投影字打在地面或牆上，並朝最近的觀者重新對位。
