VGSS · 001 · OVERVIEW

VR Gallery Spatial System

虛擬實境美術館・空間設計準則 · 30 cards · 2 tools
Overview · 總覽
README.html

A design system for the spatial design of VR museums and galleries — materials × light, ergonomics, whitespace, exhibit reserves, curves, virtual-art constructs, and a working plan-and-elevation planner. All rules calibrated for a 130 cm VR avatar with eye-height 130 cm.

所有規則以 130 公分高 VR avatar（視覺高度 130 公分）為基準。

Colors · 色彩
4 cards

Four spatial-role colours anchor the palette: env (#689F38) surrounding nature · body (#EEEEEE) gallery shell · cool (#E8EAF6) shaded rooms · warm (#FFF8E1) intimate rooms. Each is documented as a target; the materials × light card prescribes the perceived actual values.

Type · 字體
1 card

TASA Orbiter Deck for Latin & numerals (商業字型, fallback Sora). 思源黑體 (Noto Sans TC) for Chinese (OFL). JetBrains Mono for dimensions and tokens. Tabular numerals on every dimension.

Materials · 材質
3 cards

Surfaces grouped by reflectance: matte (limestone / plaster / concrete / rammed earth) · satin & glossy (oak / marble / polished concrete / ink-stone) · plus a same-material × four lights comparison.

Lighting · 光源
3 cards

Multi-light rigs (3-point / 4-point / gallery wash / spot + ambient), artistic non-real fill recipes, and the light whitespace strategy (pitch-black / silhouette / wash / gradient + transition compatibility matrix).

Shaders · 渲染器
1 card

Default stack: Mochie's Standard for body architecture, lilToon for stylised works, VRC Light Volumes for world illumination, VR Stage Lighting for projected / volumetric.

Ergonomics · 空間人因
4 cards

Avatar baseline, viewing distance bands, viewing cone (±15° focus / ±30° comfort / ±60° peripheral), and mounting height (centroid @ 130 cm).

Whitespace · 留白
2 cards

Inter-exhibit spacing (pair 40 / comfort 90 / breath 180 / silence 360 cm) and grouping rhythm (within-group ≤ 40 / between-group ≥ 180).

Curves · 曲線
1 card

Five approved radii: R 5 edge softening · R 20 furniture · R 60 alcove · R 120 room corner · R 240 vault.

Exhibit reserves · 展品量體
2 cards

Four volume sizes — S (30×30×50) · M (60×60×100) · L (120×120×150) · XL (240×240×240) cm — for stress-testing layouts before art is placed.

Room shapes · 房型
2 cards

Non-rectangular archetypes (L · T · hex · oval · curved partition · multi-room suite) plus the dedicated circular building + two fan corridors + liminal strategy. 扇形展廊以「圓形外殼」為前提，由內部 partition 切出。

Virtual light & particles
3 cards

VR-native emission taxonomy, the emission budget (spill ≤ 20% body wall), and the particles density × behaviour matrix with VR-comfort caps.

Interactives · 互動
2 cards

Reach zones (grab 55 / near 120 / far 300 cm) for objects the avatar can pick up, orbit, or nudge — and the float-height bands (low 40 / eye 130 / high 200 cm).

Information · 資訊浮層
1 card

Three display ranges: near label (80 cm, on gaze) · mid context (180 cm, on dwell) · far ambient sky-text (400 cm, always visible).

Sample drawings · 平面・立面圖
2 cards

Worked examples at 1:50 and 1:60: a 14 × 9 m linear-axis gallery (plan) and a 10 m wall section (elevation) showing the system at production scale.

Tools · 工具
2 self-contained apps

Two single-file React apps. Open directly in any modern browser. Each is ~7 MB, fully offline-capable after first load.

VGSS · 002
Spatial Planner
空間規劃工具
Plan + elevation with reserves (S/M/L/XL), walls (solid · voile · glass) with occlusion, lights (8 mount types, draggable in both views) with shadow + single-bounce reflections, room shapes (rect · L · T · hex · oval · corridor · circle · half-fan), and validation panel. Scene auto-saves to localStorage; export/import as JSON.
Open
VGSS · 003
Falloff Predictor
光衰減預估
Predict illuminance, rendered colour, and ΔE drift before baking. Pick light × surface × falloff model (inverse-square · smooth · linear · constant), tune I₀ / range / distance / target lux. Step-by-step verdict with one-click apply: re-light vs re-material decisions made explicit.
Open
SKILL.md · agent skill
For Claude Code / API use

如果你要在 Claude Code 或其他 agent 環境使用此系統，SKILL.md is the entry point. It declares the skill metadata (name, description, user-invocable) and tells the agent how to read this system when designing VR gallery spaces.

Find SKILL.md at the root of the bundle. Drop the whole folder into a skills directory and the agent will load README.html / preview cards / colors_and_type.css as needed. 把整個資料夾放到 skills 目錄即可。

Key files for agents:
README.html — visual overview · colors_and_type.css — design tokens · preview/*.html — individual cards · tools/*.html — bundled apps.
