Virtual Light · 虛擬光源類別

emission types for VR art

Point glow

點光源・燭光

Round-falloff source. Floats or sits on surface. Hero pieces.

Edge / line

線光・邊光

Glowing outline along a surface or volume edge. Defines shape, not space.

Volumetric / god-ray

體積光・光束

Visible beam through a particle medium. Implies window, gap, beam-source.

Emissive surface

面光・發光體

The artwork itself emits — a screen, panel, or glow-painted form.

Projected

投射光・投影

Cone or spot cast onto a body surface. Spill follows the wall material.
