Project T

以台9線與臺灣山區公路地景為核心的 VRChat 世界系列。 在 VR 中重建臺灣山道的質感，並圍繞社群車聚與 Time Attack 計時紀錄建立玩家俱樂部。

A VRChat world series anchored in Taiwan's mountain highways — Highway 9 and surrounding ridges. We rebuild the character of Taiwan's mountain roads in VR and grow a player community around community race nights and time-attack records.
