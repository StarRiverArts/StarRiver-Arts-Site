VR Racing Club

以 Project T 系列山道世界為賽場的 VRChat 競速社群。 玩家在台9線與各山道世界中對決計時，紀錄由 Time Attack 站統一追蹤， 並定期舉辦社群車聚與賽季活動。

A VRChat racing community that uses Project T mountain-road worlds as its circuit. Players chase each other's times across Highway 9 and other mountain-road worlds; all records are tracked through the Time Attack site, with regular race nights and seasonal events.

View track leaderboards, vehicle records, personal bests, and event results. The site is split across track, player, vehicle, event, and review sections, updated periodically by the local builder.

關於紀錄系統 About Records

紀錄分為「正式 Approved Record」與「一般 Time Attack」兩軌。 正式板需人工審核；一般板保留社群平時練習紀錄。 兩軌資料均在 TimeAttack 站內分頁呈現。

Records run on two channels: formal Approved Records (manually reviewed) and regular Time Attack (community practice runs). Both channels are presented as separate boards inside the TimeAttack site.
