Beyond GravityBeyond Gravity

Beyond Gravity 以 VRChat 為媒介，呈現天鉤（Skyhook）軌道運輸系統的尺度與運作概念。在 Space Jam 3 兩週的製作限制下，選擇以實體空間行走取代說明文字：訪客站在太空站結構中移動，用身體感受軌道高度與機械比例。

作品的核心問題是：科學概念能否在不依賴圖表和文字的情況下被空間化？天鉤站的設計回答這個問題——每個展示板的位置、高度、視角都服從敘事邏輯，而非裝飾邏輯。

Beyond Gravity uses VRChat to present the concept and scale of a Skyhook orbital transport system. Within the two-week constraint of Space Jam 3, the design choice was to spatialise the science rather than explain it: visitors walk through the station structure, using their body to feel orbital altitude and mechanical proportion.

The core question: can scientific concepts be spatially experienced without charts and text? The Skyhook station answers this — every display panel's position, height, and viewing angle follows narrative logic, not decorative logic.

Baked GI + reflection probes. Simulates direct orbital sunlight (high-contrast hard shadows) + Earth albedo fill.
